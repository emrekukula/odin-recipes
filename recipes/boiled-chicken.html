Boiled Chicken

Description

A recipe for boiled chicken; boiled chicken meat is called for in many other recipes, and is a wonderful basic recipe to have for many uses.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, halved - unpeeled
  • 3 carrots, cut into chunks - unpeeled
  • 2 stalks celery, cut into chunks
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • Water to cover

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a large pot with onion, carrots, celery and peppercorns.
  2. Add water to cover. Cover pot and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to a gentle boil and cook for about 90 minutes, or until chicken meat is falling off of the bone.
  4. Remove chicken, let cool and shred or chop the meat.

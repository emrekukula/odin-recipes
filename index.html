Odin Recipes

Favorite dish Description

A recipe for boiled chicken; boiled chicken meat is called for in many other recipes, and is a wonderful basic recipe to have for many uses.

Ingredients

    1(3 pound) whole chicken 1 large onion, halved - unpeeled 3 carrots, cut into chunks - unpeeled 2 stalks celery, cut into chunks 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns water to cover
